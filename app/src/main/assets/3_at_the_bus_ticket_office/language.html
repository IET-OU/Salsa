Language tips

Stress

Stress plays an important role in spoken English. Sometimes changing the stressed word can change the meaning.

Listen to these two questions. Can you hear what the difference is?

  1. The bus is at 19:05. Will you make that?
  2. The bus is at 19:05. Will you make that?

Answers

Here is another example. What are the meanings of these sentences with the stress placed on different words?

  1. "No I can't miss that one!"
  2. "No I can't miss that one!"
  3. "No I can't miss that one!"
