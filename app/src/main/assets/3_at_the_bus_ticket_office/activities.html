Activities

Activity 1

Listen for stressed words in English around you. Do you hear any words that have extra stress? Why do you think they have extra stress?

Examples

"Should I get the two fifteen bus?"

"No, you should get the two thirty bus."

Activity 2

In the dialogue, the ticket officer makes a recommendation to Erica about buses. Read notices or leaflets around the ticket office. See if you can find any recommendations. Think about the language. Do they always use ‘should’?

Activity 3

Ask for some advice about public transport. You could go to the ticket office, or ask a bus driver.

Try some of these phrases to help you:

Should I...?

Would it be better if...?

Is it faster to...?

What if I...?
