In this activity, Erica gets some advice about bus travel to Luton Airport. She thinks about a number of possibilities and finds out the best option.

You will learn how to talk about possibilities and conditions using would (Conditional 2), think about the importance of stress, and learn some more useful words about travel.

Get started

Think about journeys that you take. Then think of different ways to make the same journey. Say the alternatives using could to yourself, or practice with a friend or member of your family.

I could take the number 7 bus or I could go on foot.

I could get off at Central Milton Keynes or I could get off at the railway station.

If it was raining, what would you do?

Example: If it was raining I would take the number 7 bus.
