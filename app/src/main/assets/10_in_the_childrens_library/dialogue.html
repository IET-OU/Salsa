Dialogue

Toddler time

Erica tells us about her visit to a toddler group with Maria. She talks to another mother and about childcare and has to apologise for Maria's behaviour.

She and the other mother use a lot of informal language in their conversation. Look out for it and see if you can think of more formal alternatives.

After listening, use the transcript to help you understand it. You can also learn from the vocabulary.
