Language tips

Compound nouns

Compound nouns are nouns made of two or more words. The words are very closely connected and together they make a specific meaning.

In English, the first word is always stressed.

painkiller

wheelchair

Often itâ€™s easy to see compound nouns because they are written as one word (e.g. wheelchair) or with a hyphen (e.g. t-shirt).

In the dialogue, side effect is a compound noun. Which word is stressed?

Even if the compound noun is written as two words, it is important for the stress to be on the first word. Sometimes this is the only thing that makes a compound noun different from a normal adjective + noun combination.
