Activities

Activity 1

If you are sitting in a café, listen to the conversations around you. See if you can understand what people are talking about by listening for key words.

Think about the pattern of their conversation. Is it question-answer-question-answer?

Activity 2

Tell a story in English to someone you know. Like Erica, it could be about something interesting that happened in your day..

Try to use a verb in the past perfect (had done). Remember, the past perfect is for events that happen before the main events in your story.
