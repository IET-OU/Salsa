In this activity Erica and her daughter go out to a café. They get themselves coffee, use the internet, and have a nice conversation with another customer.

You will learn how to use the past perfect tense (had done), ask for WiFi and to start and continue conversations.

Get started

Visit a café by yourself or with a friend. Think about the opportunities to use English when you are there. Try one of these:

  • Listen to conversations around you
  • Speak to the staff - ask a question or make a special request
  • Read notices or adverts on the walls
  • Read a book or a newspaper
  • Start a conversation with another customer! Start by reading our tips about icebreakers.
