Grammar

Past perfect

Read or listen to the text again. Notice the use of verb tenses like this:

“I remembered I had forgotten to get the WiFi password.”

“The girl had already written it down for me.”

“Maria had not woken up.”

This tense is called the past perfect.

  • It is formed with had + past participle
  • It is used for action in the past that is completed before another action in the past.

Often, the past perfect appears with 'already'.

The girl had already written it down for me.

Practice

Complete the sentence with the correct form of the past perfect. Think about why the past perfect is being used.

  4. I realised that I ___ ____ (leave) my wallet at home
