5 Activities

Activity 1

Read the dialogue and the vocabulary again. It will help you complete the following useful phrases.

  1. I'm really sorry to ______ you.
  2. I would like to ______ a problem in my neighbourhood.
  3. How do I __ _____ making a complaint?
  4. Can you please ______ it __ soon?

You can try to use these words and phrases in the next activities.

Answers

  1. bother
  2. report
  3. go about
  4. follow it up

Activity 2

Think about the word complaint. In the dialogue it is used with different verbs (e.g make a complaint).

Look at the dialogue again. What are these verbs? What do the phrases mean? How are they different to 'make a complaint'?

Activity 3

Is something wrong in your area. Holes in the road? Unpleasant neighbours? Or maybe there aren’t enough facilities like parks for your children. Discuss the problems with a friend or neighbour. Or you could make some notes and discuss them in your next language class.

Internet only!

Look at the council’s website. Decide whether the council can help you with your problem or not.

Activity 4 - internet only!

Are there any words or topics on the council’s website that you don’t understand? Look them up, or take them to your next language class and find out their meaning.
