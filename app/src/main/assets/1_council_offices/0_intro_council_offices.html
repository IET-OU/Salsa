In this activity, Erica visits her local council offices to complain about some graffiti that has appeared on her street. She doesn't quite get the response she is hoping for though.

You will learn how to politely make a request in a formal conversation, give information and understand how others are going to act on it.

Warm up

Think about the following important words that are associated with the council:

  • Councillor
  • Local
  • Community
  • Facility
  • Services

Do you know the meaning of them all?

Think about what it is that your council does for you. Compare what you know about your council in the UK with what there is in your home country.

Activity

Write down in English all the things that you know your local council does for you. Compare your list with your classmates'.
