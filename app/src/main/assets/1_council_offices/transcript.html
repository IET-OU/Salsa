Reporting a problem

Customer services: Hello there, thank you for coming in. How can we help you today?

Erica: Hello, good morning. I’m sorry to bother you but I would like to report some nasty graffiti on my street. It’s right outside my house. How could I go about getting it removed?

Customer services: Ok, if you leave the complaint with us we may be able to follow it up within 24 hours if it’s an emergency. Can you describe it for me? Is it obscene, racist or religious in nature?

Erica: No it isn’t really any of those, but it’s big and ugly and I don’t like the atmosphere it creates in my neighbourhood.

Customer services: I’m sorry, if it isn’t obscene or offensive then it doesn’t count as emergency graffiti. I’m afraid we won’t be able to remove it immediately, but we will try to remove it as soon as we can.

Erica: I see. That’s disappointing. I've been putting up with it for a very long time and I was hoping you could remove it soon.

Customer services: I’m sorry we can’t do more. Obviously our cleaning teams are very busy. Bear with me please. I’ll take your details and log the complaint for you.
