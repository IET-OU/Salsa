In this activity, Erica visits her local council offices to complain about some graffiti that has appeared on her street. She doesn't quite get the response she is hoping for though.

You will practise how to politely make a request in a formal conversation, give information and understand how others are going to act on it.

Get started

Look around you while you are waiting in the council office. Read signs, posters or leaflets to learn about your council.

You may find some important words to do with your council. Do you know what they mean? Can you find out?
