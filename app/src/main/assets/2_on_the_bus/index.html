In this lesson, Erica tries to travel by bus around Milton Keynes. Listen to her story about trying to ask for directions. She has a few problems with pronunciation of place names!

You will practise reading a narrative, practise using reported speech and learn some useful words for bus travel.

Get started

Look at a bus map of your local area. Do you know how to pronounce the place names around you?

If you aren't sure, ask someone near you how to say the places.

"Excuse me, how do you pronounce this place?"
