Activities

Activity 1

Read the signs on the bus or around the bus stop. Take notice of some of the language constructions and think about them.

Here are some examples:

Bus stopping

Mind the doors

Get on board

Concessionary fare

Passengers are reminded to take all their belongings with them.

This service is usually operated using a wheelchair accessible vehicle.

There is no guarantee that there will be space on a vehicle at any given time.

Do you find the language you see easy to understand? If not, take some phrases to class and discuss them, or look them up on the internet.

Activity 2

Tell a friend about a conversation that you had today. You will have to practise reported speech.

For example:

The teacher said we had to...

I met a lady who said she was travelling to...

Activity 3 - internet only!

Visit the Milton Keynes Council bus travel page.

Plan a journey to an interesting place that you would like to visit.
