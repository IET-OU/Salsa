Language tips

Sounds in connected speech

When people speak naturally, many of the sounds connect or join together.

Read this section of the dialogue. Where do you think the words will connect?

Andrei: Yeah, I had been studying for years in Poland before I came here. I learned enough to get a job as a mechanic. I have a degree in engineering from Poland but I moved here because the economy is good.

Next, listen to the speech to hear if you're correct.

Answer

In many of these connections, you can hear that the consonant at the end of one word disappears. You can only hear one consonant, at the start of the next word.

Polan_ before

Some times the consonant at the end of one word changes to be like the consonant at the start of the next word.

im Poland

You will also notice that some consonants change when the next word starts with a vowel.
