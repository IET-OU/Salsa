In this activity, Erica talks to Andrei, a new classmate on her English course. They have a conversation about where they are from and the things they are doing in the UK.

You will learn how to use question tags to keep a conversation open. You will also have a look at how pronunciation sometimes changes in connected speech.

Get started

Listen to a casual conversation between two people at your college or workplace. Note down any words or phrases you hear that will help you in conversations.

For example:

  • "What/how about you?"
  • "You're new on this course, right?"
  • "What are you up to?"
  • "Wow, that's so good!"
  • "Oh no, that's awful!"
  • "Sure!"
