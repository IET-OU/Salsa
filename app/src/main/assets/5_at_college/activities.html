Activities

Activity 1

Find out something from someone in college. Ask them where they were born or what other courses they are doing.

Think about using question tags to invite the person to comment or agree with you. It might help keep the conversation going.

Activity 2

Listen to people asking questions in English. Pay attention to the intonation they use.

Listen out for question tags. Does the voice go up or go down at the end?

Activity 3

Look out for long words on signs, notices or posters around you. Guess how you pronounce them. Pay attention to where the stress goes.

Write down a list, and then find out from somebody how to pronounce the words. Or you can use a dictionary to check pronunciation and stress.

Did you get the stress in the right place?
