Activities

Activity 1

Can you think of synonyms (words that mean the same as) for some of these words or phrases. Erica uses some of them in her story. Which are informal and which are more informal?

  1. to give something back
  2. to get your money back
  3. to swap
  4. not perfectly made
  5. a thing, an object
  6. to sign up
  7. to say no

Answers

Activity 2

Do you know any English idioms? Write down your favourites and share them with friends.

Collect some more idioms! Look out for interesting expressions and write them down.

Concentrate on listening for and understanding idioms. It can be difficult to use them naturally in conversations.

Activity 3

Listen for people politely saying no to invitations or offers. Or listen out for the use of “I’m afraid”. Try to copy them. Pay attention to their intonation. Polite intonation is as important as the words.
