SALSA is an Open University project exploring location-triggered language learning through the use of smartphones and Bluetooth LE technology. Beacons is the project app.

Funding

SALSA is an MK:Smart Open Challenge Call winner. It is funded by The Open University's PVC RSQ to open new lines of inquiry and application to capitalise on MK:Smart research. The SALSA Beacons app content has been supported by The Open University's Higher Education Innovation Fund.
