Using the pool

In this conversation, Erica finds out a lot about using the swimming pool and also about doing other activities. As you read or listen, look out for uses of conditional sentences like If I... I go... or unless I... I won't.... You will practise these later in the activity.

After listening, use the transcript to help you understand it. You can also learn from the vocabulary.
