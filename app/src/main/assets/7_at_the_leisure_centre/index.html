In this activity, Erica goes to the local leisure centre to use the swimming pool for the first time. She gets help to understand what she can and can’t do.

You will practise forming conditional sentences with if, unless and as long as. You will also be reminded about when to use the passive.

Get started

When you’re at the leisure centre, find an activity that you want to know more about.

Read a leaflet about it or ask someone.
