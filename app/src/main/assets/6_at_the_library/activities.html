Activities

Activity 1

Listen to people at the library asking questions. How do they introduce their requests? Do they use phrases like “I was just wondering…”? Write down any more phrases that you hear.

Listen to their intonation. Does the voice go up or down?

Activity 2

Listen to the start of Erica’s story again. There are three words with a syllable that is spelled but not spoken (e.g. comfortable -> “comftable”). Can you find them?

Answers

Listen to the way Erica says these words and repeat them to yourself.

Activity 3 – internet only!

Visit the Milton Keynes library web page. Find out what the eLibrary is. What does it say about learning English with the eLibrary?

Activity 4

Visit your nearest library. Find out what other things can help you learn English. For example there might be ‘easy readers’, dual language books or books with CDs.
