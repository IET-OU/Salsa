Grammar

Prepositions

  1. C on: used for days of the week, or dates
  2. D for: used to express how long something lasts
  3. H ago: used to say how much time has passed since something happened
  4. F before: at or during a time earlier than another time
  5. A until: up to a particular time
  6. G by: not later than
  7. B from: used to show the time when something starts
  8. E since: from a particular time in the past, up to a later time or the present time

Language tips

Polite questions

  • “I was wondering, how…”
  • “Could I ask you – what do I…?”
  • “I also wanted to ask …”

Clarifying information

  • “So you mean I don’t have to…?”
  • “Is that right?”
  • “I just need to…?”
  • “Can I just check that…?”
