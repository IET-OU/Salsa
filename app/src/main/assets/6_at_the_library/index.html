In this activity, Erica tells us about a visit to her local library. She finds out how to register and discovers all the things she can do there.

You will practise finding out about local services, clarifying information that you hear and making polite enquiries.

Get started

Look around the library. Find out about an event that’s happening. It could be in the library or somewhere else. Find out:

  • What the event is
  • Where/when it is
  • Who it’s for
  • How long the event is

If you are interested in the event, ask for more information. It’s good practice.

Go to Language tips to practise asking for information.
