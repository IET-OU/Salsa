Time words

Erica uses time words when she expresses time in her story. Remind yourself what these time words are.

Can you match the preposition with the explanation? Look back at the dialogue to help you.

  1. on
  2. for
  3. ago
  4. before
  5. until
  6. by
  7. from
  8. since
  1. up to a particular time
  2. used to show the time when something starts
  3. used for days of the week, or dates
  4. used to express how long something lasts
  5. from a particular time in the past, up to a later time or the present time
  6. at or during a time earlier than another time
  7. not later than
  8. used to say how much time has passed since something happened
