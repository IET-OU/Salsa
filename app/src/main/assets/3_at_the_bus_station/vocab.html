2 Vocabulary

recommend (v)

Say what you think is a good thing to do. Tell someone what the best option is.

I recommend you leave earlier so you don't miss the connection.

you had best

The best thing you could do is... I recommend that you should...

You had best buy your tickets now because it's cheaper.

hop on (v)

Another way to say ‘get on’ or enter a bus. The opposite is ‘hop off’.

To get to Luton Airport you should hop on the 99 bus.

connection (n)

The next bus you get onto when you have to change buses on a journey.

We had to wait a while for our connection at Milton Keynes Central.

reduced service (n)

When buses are running less frequently than normal, maybe because it is the weekend or holiday .

There is a reduced bus service on bank holidays.

plenty of time

Lots of time. Enough time.

If you leave early you will have plenty of time to get the bus to Luton.

Will you make that?

Will you have enough time? Will you be able to get there?
‘Make the bus’ is the opposite of ‘miss the bus’.

The last bus is at 20.05. Will you make it?

It will be tight

It will be close. There may not be enough time. I might not make it.

I think I'll make the last bus, but if my flight is delayed it will be tight!
